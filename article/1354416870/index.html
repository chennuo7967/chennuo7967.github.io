Mar 6, Sunday, sunny

When I got up this morning, I saw that my diary book got wet! My grandma told me she wanted to close the window last night, but she put off the flower bottle, then all of things on the table got wet. So you can see I’m using a new book to keep diary.

I like listening to music, especially Cantonese songs. My favorite singers are Beyond and Tat Ming Pair. They wrote many classical songs, such as “”, “”, “” and etc.. However, their style are quite different.

Beyond’s songs are very powerful or happy, their songs encourage us a lot.

But Tat Ming Pair’s sounds more sadly, even negative. Maybe they are always in the corner of HK music society.

Nowadays, the songs are not the songs. The classical songs won’t be back again.

Mar 7, Monday, sunny

Its’ the fourth week of the new term, and the first exam will take place in the seventh week. Now I have to start the process of going over the lessons.

Tomorrow is the Women’s Day and all of female teachers are going to play outside tomorrow afternoon. So we only have math and physics in the afternoon. Also, there will be a breaking for 40 minute. Before the class begins, we’ll say, “Happy Women’s Day!” to the teachers. If we do the same thing on the math lesson, what would happen ?

Mar 8, Tuesday, Sunny

Today is the Women’s Day, so all of female teachers played outside this afternoon. Also, all of lessons in the afternoon were taught by the male teachers. As what I said yesterday, before the math lesson,

“Class begin !”

“Good afternoon, class !”

“Happy Women’s Day! H2S !”

On the history lesson today, it’s not quiet as usual. Because Peng talked about the Culture Revolution Then, you must know what they said. I don’t want to write down here, because it’s too dangerous.

Then, we did an experiment on the physics lesson. Xian showed us a video. He stood on the eggs, but the egg didn’t broken! But he play a video of his failure. The eggs were in pieces! As a result, there was much more eggs in our lunch soup.
